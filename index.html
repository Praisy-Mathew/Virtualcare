    Emergency Department

    Our Emergency Department offers immediate virtual care for critical health issues, available 24/7. Our team of experienced professionals is ready to handle all types of emergencies.

    Services: Trauma care, cardiac emergencies, stroke treatment, etc

    Paediatric Department

    Our Paediatric Department provides comprehensive virtual care for infants, children, and adolescents. From routine checkups to specialized treatments, we are dedicated to the health and well-being of your child.

    Services: Vaccinations, routine check-ups, specialized paediatric treatments

    Orthopaedics Department

    Our Orthopaedic Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal conditions through virtual consultations. Whether dealing with a sports injury, arthritis, or chronic pain, our experts are here to help.

    Services: Joint replacement, sports injuries, fracture treatment

    Gynaecology Department

    Our Gynaecology Department offers a full range of virtual women’s health services, including prenatal care, reproductive health, and menopause management. Our team is committed to providing personalized and compassionate care.

    Services: Prenatal care, reproductive health, menopause management

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our services and how to use our platform.

    • How do I book a virtual appointment?

      You can book a virtual appointment by visiting our booking page, selecting your preferred department, and filling out the required details.

    • What do I need for a virtual consultation?

      You need a stable internet connection, a device with a camera and microphone, and a quiet place for your consultation.

    • How do I receive my prescriptions?

      After your consultation, the doctor will send your prescription to our partner pharmacy, and you will receive your medications at home.

    • Can I get a referral to a specialist?

      Yes, our doctors can refer you to specialists for advanced care. We will coordinate the referral process for you.
